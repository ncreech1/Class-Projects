In case Topcoder's servers are down, here is a summary of the problem:
  • You are given a vector of integers, arr.
  • The integers are distinct, positive, and in increasing order.
  • The topcoder constraints have the size of arr being between 1 and 50, but you can solve this for array sizes up to 1,000,000.
  • For any two integers a ≤ b, the "range [a,b]" is composed of all integers i such that a ≤ i ≤ b.
  • If you view arr as a union of ranges, what is the smallest number of ranges that are required, so that their union is arr.
